The function has a single parameter named m with the following content:
- yourGuesses: Your previous guesses and their results (array with items {g, r})
- opponentsGuesses: Opponents previous guesses and their results (array with items {g, r})
content of r: 1 => actual > g, -1 => actual < g
content of g: Guessed nr
m can also be used to store state between guesses
